About Us

Our company namely Pan Digital Technology Sdn Bhd. register and incorporated in Malaysia since 25th February 2000.We are a trading Company supplying a wide range of Machinery & Equipment in the field of Plastic Manufacturing.

We are one of the leading suppliers for plastics join and seal machinery in the country. Our type of machine was generally applied welding on plastics injection, plastic or P.E. laminated sheet / tube, fabric and non-woven. We also specialise in Ultrasonic custom horn & jig design & Maintenance.

We have supplied and services to major plastics assembly Industry like Automotive, Medication, Packaging, Textile & Garments, Electronic & Electrical, Filter Product, Sanitary & Toiletry Industry, Safety & Pharmaceutical Industry, Household Product, P.V.C & Canvas, Cosmetic & beauty and etc..
